Military

Military bases

Major U.S. naval bases in Japan include US Fleet Activities Yokosuka (home to the Seventh Fleet), U.S. Fleet Activities Sasebo, and Naval Air Facility Atsugi (co-located with JMSDF), with significant presence in Okinawa via White Beach and Camp Foster; Japan Maritime Self-Defense Force (JMSDF) also operates key bases like Yokosuka, Maizuru, and Kure, supporting their own fleet and joint operations.

Military Aircraft stationed in the past and present

In the present, F-15C/J fighters, F/A-18 Hornets, and various support aircraft like KC-135 tankers and E-3 airborne warning systems


In the past,F-14 Tomcats were stationed in Japan, specifically with the U.S. Navy's VF-154 "Black Knights" squadron at Naval Air Station (NAS) Atsugi from 1991 to 2003, as they were forward-deployed with Carrier Air Wing Five (CVW-5) on carriers like the USS Independence and USS Kitty Hawk, operating out of Yokosuka and Atsugi
